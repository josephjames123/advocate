Security Laws

SEBI 2015

Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements

These regulations lay down the listing obligations for entities listed on stock exchanges. It includes disclosure requirements, corporate governance norms, and obligations to ensure transparency and protection of investors' interests.

SEBI 2011

Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers

This regulation is designed to regulate substantial acquisitions of shares and takeovers of companies. It aims to ensure transparency, fair treatment of shareholders, and protection of their interests during such transactions.

SEBI 2003

Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices Relating to Securities Market

: These regulations prohibit fraudulent and unfair trade practices in the securities market. They define and penalize activities such as market manipulation, misleading statements, and fraudulent transactions to protect the integrity of the securities market.
