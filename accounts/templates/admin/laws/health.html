Health Laws

IPC Section 272

Adulteration of Food or Drink Intended for Sale

This section deals with the adulteration of food or drink intended for sale, making it a criminal offense. Adulteration poses risks to public health, and this section aims to prevent such practices.

IPC Section 273

Sale of Noxious Food or Drink:

This section criminalizes the sale of noxious (harmful) food or drink, with the intent to cause injury to human health. It addresses situations where substances harmful to health are knowingly sold as food or drink.

Section 443:

The Food Safety and Standards Act

Governs food safety standards, regulation of food products, and establishes the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).

Section 487

The Drugs and Cosmetics Act

Regulates the import, manufacture, distribution, and sale of drugs and cosmetics. It ensures the quality and safety of pharmaceutical products.
