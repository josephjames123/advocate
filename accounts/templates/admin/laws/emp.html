Employment Laws

Section 499

Defamation

Defamation laws could be applicable in situations where false statements or publications harm the reputation of an individual or an organization, impacting employment relationships.

Section 503

Criminal Intimidation

Deals with criminal intimidation, which might be relevant in cases of threats or intimidation in the employment context.

Section 383

Extortion

This section deals with the offense of extortion, which may be applicable if an employee uses force, threats, or intimidation to obtain something of value from the employer or a colleague.

Section 406

Punishment for Criminal Breach of Trust

n an employment context, it may be applicable if an employee, entrusted with property or finances, breaches that trust by misusing or misappropriating the assets of the employer.
