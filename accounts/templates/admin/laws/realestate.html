Real Estate Laws

Section 406

Punishment for Criminal Breach of Trust

Criminal breach of trust may be relevant in real estate transactions where someone entrusted with property, funds, or documents misappropriates or breaches that trust.

IPC Section 467

Forgery of valuable security, will, etc.:

This section deals with the forgery of valuable security, which could be relevant in cases where forged documents related to real estate transactions are used.

Section 418:

cheating with knowledge

Deals with cheating with knowledge that wrongful loss may ensue to a person whose interest the offender is bound to protect.

Act, 2016 (RERA):

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act

Regulates the real estate sector, promotes transparency, and protects the interests of homebuyers.
