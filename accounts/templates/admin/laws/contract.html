Section 420

Cheating and Dishonestly Inducing Delivery of Property

This section deals with cheating and dishonestly inducing the delivery of property. In the context of contracts, it could be applicable if a person, through deceptive means, induces another to enter into a contract, leading to a financial loss.

Section 415

Cheating

This section pertains to the general offense of cheating, which may be relevant in cases where fraudulent activities are involved in the formation or execution of a contract.
